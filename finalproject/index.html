Site Purpose

This website will provide users with weather infoemation at all times

Scenarios

General public/everyday users: Sarah checks the app each morning to decide what to wear for work and plan her day accordingly.

Travelers/commuters: Mike uses the app before his daily commute to check road conditions and plan the best departure time.

Color Schema

Primary Color (#4A90E2): Used for headers, buttons, and key interactive elements to create visual hierarchy and draw attention.

Secondary Color (#F5F5F5): Used for backgrounds and sections to provide contrast and improve readability.

Typography

Font Family: Arial, sans-serif

Application: Arial is used throughout the site plan for both headings and body text. This font ensures readability across all devices and provides a clean, professional appearance suitable for weather information display.
